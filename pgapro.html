Meet the Professional

Darran Clark PGA Golf Professional I started playing at Mountain Ash Golf Club in 1984 and, as a junior, played for Glamorgan boys and represented Wales in the European Schools Championship in 1993.

I began my PGA training in 1994 at Moor Allerton Golf Club and qualified in 1998 while at Whitchurch Club. I have been Head Professional at Peterstone Lakes Golf Club, Mountain Ash Golf Club, and Aberdare Golf Club.

I have always enjoyed competing and have five wins as a professional. I have over 25 years of coaching experience and taught at every level, from new2golf schemes to European Tour members.

About Our Club

Established in 1905, Caerphilly Golf Club occupies a prime location near Caerphilly town center, with easy access to transportation. This 18-hole course offers scenic views of the town and countryside. Beyond golf, it serves as an ideal venue for weddings and special occasions, with a spacious function room, lounge, and patio overlooking the fairways. Additional amenities include ample parking, table planning, decoration services, and the option for a disco. The club also caters to businesses, providing rooms for meetings, seminars, and exhibitions.
