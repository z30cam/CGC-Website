Meet the Pro

Darren started playing at Mountain Ash Golf Club in 1984 and, as a junior, played for Glamorgan boys and represented Wales in the European Schools Championship in 1993.

He began his PGA training in 1994 at Moor Allerton Golf Club and qualified in 1998 while at Whitchurch Club. He has been Head Professional at Peterstone Lakes Golf Club, Mountain Ash Golf Club, and Aberdare Golf Club.

Darran has always enjoyed competing and has five wins as a professional. He has over 25 years of coaching experience and has taught at every level, from new2golf schemes to European Tour members.

Coaching

Darran's passion is golf, both as a player and a coach. He finds fulfillment in helping students improve their game, whether introducing beginners to the joy of a well-struck shot or assisting competitive players in preparing for their next competition. Golf is a sport that doesn't conform to a "one size fits all" approach. Each golfer has a distinct physical makeup, leading to unique golf swings. A comprehensive understanding of your golf swing paves the way to a more successful game.

The Pro Shop

In the shop, you'll find a selection of men's and women's clothing from renowned brands such as Footjoy, Ping, and Under Armour. We stock a variety including sweaters, polo shirts, and trousers. Additionally, we always have waterproof apparel in stock to ensure you stay dry on the course.

To enhance your experience, we offer a range of equipment options for hire. Whether you prefer pull, electric trolleys, or ride-on buggies, we have you covered. We recommend booking these in advance to secure your preferred choice.

Regarding golf clubs, we take pride in offering an impressive collection of the latest models from leading manufacturers. Feel free to try them out before purchasing, ensuring you find the perfect fit for your game.
